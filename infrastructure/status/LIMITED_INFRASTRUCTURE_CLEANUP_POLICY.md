# LIMITED INFRASTRUCTURE CLEANUP POLICY

PROJECT:
INFRASTRUCTURE_REORGANIZATION

UNIT:
INFRA-0070

STATUS:
DRAFT

## Scope

This project may only clean infrastructure material that is already centralized, verified, and no longer needed locally.

## Explicitly Protected Material

Do not delete:

- OPTIPLEX photos
- OPTIPLEX music
- imported photos from other computers
- imported music from other computers
- unclassified media collections
- playable or viewable media collections
- any material needed for future photo organization
- any material needed for future music authority, deduplication, or migration projects

## Allowed Cleanup Candidate Categories

The following may be inventoried as cleanup candidates:

- redundant local Git repository clones
- obsolete recovery folders
- temporary infrastructure staging folders
- duplicated project bootstrap files
- abandoned technical transition folders
- already-centralized infrastructure copies

## Rule

Inventory first.

Review second.

Owner approval third.

Deletion only after explicit approval.

## Current Decision

Photo cleanup is out of scope.

Music cleanup is out of scope.

Media deduplication is out of scope.

Music migration is out of scope.

Limited infrastructure cleanup may proceed only through documented candidate review.
