### Source Effectiveness
SRC0002 proved to be a high-yield source for Colombian Tropical music, particularly Cumbia and Salsa. The use of official shop product pages provided high-confidence evidence for tracklists and artist credits. However, the shop catalog is compilation-heavy, leading to high overlap in later batches (Batch 0005).

### Remaining Known Inventory
- Approximately 10 low-value releases remain in the identified inventory (seasonal or very small tracklists).

### Lessons Learned
1. **Compilation Overlap:** Compilations (USB "100 Exitos") provided rapid growth but required robust duplicate detection.
2. **Artist Text:** Discos Fuentes often uses "Varios Artistas" or non-standardized group names; manual review remains essential for artist promotion.
3. **Evidence Persistence:** Linking directly to shop product URLs worked well for traceability.
