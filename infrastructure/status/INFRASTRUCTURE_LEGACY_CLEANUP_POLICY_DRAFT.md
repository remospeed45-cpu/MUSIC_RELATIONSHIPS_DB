# INFRASTRUCTURE LEGACY CLEANUP POLICY (DRAFT)

PROJECT: INFRASTRUCTURE_REORGANIZATION
UNIT: INFRA-0030

STATUS:
DRAFT

Purpose:

Define which asset categories must remain on source systems after consolidation and which categories may be removed after verification.

## Photos

Policy:

- Copy to BOSGAME.
- Preserve OPTIPLEX source collection.
- Preserve LENOVO source collections until separately reviewed.
- No deletion approved.

Reason:

Photos remain active-access content and may be needed locally.

## Music

Policy:

- No migration currently approved.
- Preserve OPTIPLEX music collection.
- Preserve source collections.

Reason:

OPTIPLEX remains playback and processing location.

## Project Repositories

Policy:

- BOSGAME is approved long-term repository storage location.
- Source copies may be removed after verification and explicit approval.

## Foundation

Policy:

- BOSGAME Foundation location is canonical.
- Legacy copies may be removed after verification and explicit approval.

## Research PDFs

Policy:

- BOSGAME research library is canonical.
- Legacy copies may be removed after verification and explicit approval.

## Generated Artifacts

Policy:

- Large generated artifacts belong in PROJECT_DATA.
- Git repositories should not be long-term storage for large generated outputs.

## General Rule

Copy completion alone does not authorize deletion.

Deletion requires:

1. Verification.
2. Documented approval.
3. Logged execution.


## Exceptions To Cleanup

### Photo Exception

Photo consolidation into BOSGAME does not authorize deletion of source photo collections.

Approved policy:

- BOSGAME receives consolidated copies.
- OPTIPLEX photo collections remain preserved.
- LENOVO photo collections remain preserved until separately reviewed.
- Future organization does not automatically authorize source deletion.

Reason:

Photos remain active-access content and may continue to be required on workstations.

### Music Exception

Music collections are exempt from cleanup until a future music-organization phase is completed.

Approved policy:

- No music migration currently approved.
- OPTIPLEX remains music playback and processing node.
- Existing music collections remain preserved.

Reason:

Music organization, authority integration, and canonical destination design remain incomplete.
